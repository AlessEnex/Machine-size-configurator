Calculation Rules

Tanks Section

  • Base Length (based on Liquid Receiver Volume):
    • 300L: 1500 mm
    • 460L: 1600 mm
    • 700L: 1900 mm
    • 1000L: 2300 mm
    • 2x570L: 2500 mm
  • Air Conditioning: Adds 1000 mm.
  • Liquid Subcooler: Adds 300 mm.
  • Two Parts Configuration: Adds 100 mm.

Compressors Section

Lower Deck Length

  • MT/Aux Compressors (2/4-Cyl): 500 mm per compressor.
  • MT/Aux Compressors (6-Cyl): 700 mm per compressor.
  • LT (6-Cyl): Adds 700 mm per compressor to this deck.
  • Oil Separator: Adds length based on the selected model.

Upper Deck Length

  • LT (2/4-Cyl): 500 mm per compressor.
  • Heat Recoveries: Adds 500 mm per unit.
  • Oil Reserve: Adds a fixed 600 mm.

Final Compressors Length

The final length for the compressors section is the longer of the Lower Deck and Upper Deck lengths.
