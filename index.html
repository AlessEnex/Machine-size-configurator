Calculation Rules

Overall Dimensions

  • Cladding: Adds 50 mm to each side (total +100 mm to length and width).
  • Cladding (Walk-in): In addition to the standard 50 mm, it adds 1000 mm to the overall width.
  • Machine Height:
    • No Cladding: Height is determined by the selected tank volume.
    • With Cladding (Yes or Yes + Walk-in): Base height + 300 mm, capped at a maximum of 2500 mm.

Tanks Section

Tanks Section Length

  • Base Length (Liquid Receiver): Calculated based on the selected volume.
    • 150L/185L: 600 mm
    • 300L/350L/460L: 800 mm
    • 700L: 1000 mm
    • 1000L: 1100 mm (for single tanks)
    • Coupled 350+310L: 1400 mm
    • Coupled 350+350L / 570+350L: 1550 mm
    • Coupled 570+570L: 1600 mm (for coupled tanks)
    • Coupled 2x430L: 2000 mm (for coupled tanks)
    • Coupled 2x1000L: 2000 mm (for coupled tanks)
  • MT Suction Accumulator: Adds length based on volume (900 mm or 1100 mm).
  • Secondary Line Accumulator: Adds 500 mm if 'Yes'.
  • Air Conditioning: Adds 800 mm if 'Yes'.
  • Two Parts Configuration: Adds 100 mm.
  • K65 Connections (in Two Parts mode): Adds an additional 100 mm.

Tanks Section Width

  • 150L/185L Receiver: 1000 mm.
  • 300L or 350L Receiver: 1100 mm.
  • All other configurations: 1200 mm.

Compressors Section

Lower Deck Length

  • MT/Aux Compressors (2/4-Cyl): 500 mm per compressor.
  • MT/Aux Compressors (6-Cyl): 700 mm per compressor.
  • LT (6-Cyl): Adds 700 mm per compressor to this deck.
  • Oil Separator: Adds length based on the selected model (e.g., 400 mm to 2000 mm).

Upper Deck Length

  • LT (2/4-Cyl): 500 mm per compressor.
  • Heat Recoveries: Adds 500 mm per unit.
  • Oil Reserve: Adds a fixed 600 mm.
  • Oil Separator: Adds length based on the selected model (to ensure final max length is correct).

Final Compressors Length

The final length is the maximum of the Lower Deck and Upper Deck lengths. An additional 100 mm is added if using K65 connections in "Two Parts" mode.

Compressors Section Width

  • Any 6-Cylinder Compressor: 1200 mm.
  • No 6-Cyl, with LT 4-Cyl: 1100 mm.
  • No 6-Cyl, with LT 2-Cyl (or no LT): 1000 mm.

Electrical Panel

  • B2B Panel: Dimensions (L, W, H) are fixed by model. "Opening" labels appear above and below the panel in 2D visualization.
  • Head Panel: Dimensions (L, W, H) are fixed by model.
    • Orientation "Aligned": Panel length is L, width is W. "Opening" label appears below the panel.
    • Orientation "Perpendicular": Panel length is W, width is L (rotated). "Opening" label appears along the right side of the panel (rotated).
    • Cladding Rule: If Cladding ("Yes" or "Yes + Walk-in") is selected, the Head Panel orientation is automatically forced to "Aligned" and cannot be changed.

2D Visualization

  • Cladding "No": Shows three configurations (B2B, Head Aligned, Head Perpendicular).
  • Cladding "Yes" or "Yes + Walk-in": Shows two configurations (B2B, Head Aligned). The Head Perpendicular option is not applicable and therefore not shown.
